Rafla Menu

From our broad menu you’ll find perfect italian pizzas with a thin and crispy crust, delicious soups, rich pastas and sugary desserts. Choose your favorites from the list, try new flavors or choose the best toppings on your pizza.



Normal sized pizzas available with a gluten-free crust (+ 2,0 €). Our gluten-free pizza crust is naturally gluten-free, egg-free and milk-free. Our pizza cheese is lactose free. Pizzas are now also available with vegan cheese (+ 1 €).



Welcome to enjoy italian restaurant atmosphere and feast with the best pizzas in town!
